# 📱 Install as Mobile App (PWA)

Your web app can now be installed on phones just like a regular app!

---

## iPhone / iPad Installation

1. **Open Safari** (important - must use Safari)
2. Go to: **http://192.168.99.95:5173**
3. Tap the **Share** button ⬆️ (square with arrow)
4. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"** ➕
5. Tap **Add** in the top right
6. The app will appear on your home screen! 🎉

---

## Android Phone Installation

1. **Open Chrome** browser
2. Go to: **http://192.168.99.95:5173**
3. You should see an **install banner** at the bottom:
   - "Add Protect Us to Home Screen"
4. Tap **INSTALL** or **Add to Home Screen**
5. The app will appear in your app drawer! 🎉

**If you don't see the banner:**
1. Tap the **3 dots** menu ⋮ in top right
2. Tap **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home Screen"**
